Meet the Authors

At RescueTails, we're passionate about all animals, and strive to make the world a better place for both us and nature. We are dedicated to educating, sharing, and loving animals of all shapes, sizes, and kinds, and keeping you informed on Austin and world news related to animals and pets. Here's the team:
